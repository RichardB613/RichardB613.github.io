Meet the team

Founded by a group of Pharmacists, Oak Clinical Solutions Ltd was formed as a UK-based Investigational Medicinal Product (IMP) manufacturing facility. The specialist team can draw on a combined total of over 50 years of experience working in the NHS, Pharmaceutical Industry, Primary Care, Community Pharmacy and Research & Development. Oak Clinical Solutions can provide support to commercial and non-commercial Sponsors for all their clinical trial study needs.

Mr Nauman Hussain BSc (1 st Class Hons), MPharm (1 st Class Hons), MRPharmS, PG Dip GPP, IPresc

Nauman is a Senior Clinical Pharmacist at a large NHS Trust. He is a highly experienced Pharmacist specializing in Critical Care Medicine and Research. Nauman has held several prominent positions including Principal Pharmacist for Clinical Services, Lead Pharmacist for Surgery and Lead Pharmacist for Anaesthetics, Critical Care, Pain Services and Theatres. He has a proven track record of delivering high quality patient-centred care and is a clinically enhanced independent prescriber. Nauman is a tutor and clinical pharmacy lecturer for Doctors, Nurses and Physiotherapists. He is actively involved in research with interests in renal replacement therapy and currently a Principal Investigator for the landmark Beta-Lactam INfusion Group study (BLINGIII) in critically ill patients. He is also an honorary advanced pharmacist practitioner at Medway School of Pharmacy, University of Kent

Mr Mohammed Zahir Rashid MPharm, MRPharmS, PG ClinDip

Zahir is an experienced clinical pharmacist with over 15 years clinical field experience. Clinically trained as a specialist oncology pharmacist with extensive experience in MHRA licensed aseptic services including CIVAS, Batch manufacture of non-sterile products as well feasibility assessment,  aseptic manufacture, QA release and administration of Phase 1-3 clinical trials  Zahir has a wealth of experience within NHS commissioning, medicines information, formulary development and specialist procurement as well as system wide medicines shortage mitigation and multistakeholder engagement

Mr Mohammed Rashid Khan MPharm, MRPharmS, PG ClinDip

Over the last decade Rashid has worked as Lead Pharmacist for Clinical Trials in two prominent NHS Trusts in London ensuring all trials of investigational medicinal products are conducted in accordance with ethics, law and in parallel with clinical hospital policies. Rashid has experience working on Phase 1, 2, 3 and 4 studies in multiple clinical areas and understands the range of complexities modern clinical trials can have; working closely with Project Managers, Clinical teams, Sponsors and other Clinical Research Organizations to deliver quality research whilst maintaining the safety of our patients and healthy volunteers. Rashid has managed a Pharmacy in a world renowned Early Phase Clinical Research Organisation, holding responsibility for the management of the Pharmacy and the preparation of IMP dosing for on-site administration
