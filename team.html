Meet the team

Founded by a group of Pharmacists, Oak Clinical Solutions Ltd was formed as a UK-based Investigational Medicinal Product (IMP) manufacturing facility. The specialist team can draw on a combined total of over 50 years of experience working in the NHS, Pharmaceutical Industry, Primary Care, Community Pharmacy and Research & Development. Oak Clinical Solutions can provide support to commercial and non-commercial Sponsors for all their clinical trial study needs

Mr Nauman Hussain BSc (Hons), MPharm (Hons), PG Dip, IPresc

Nauman has held post as a senior specialist pharmacist at a large NHS trust. He is a highly experienced clinician specialising in research and intensive care medicine. He has been Principal Investigator for a number of international clinical trials and is passionate about bringing applied research to under-served communities. He is also an honorary practitioner at a leading academic institute.

Mr Mohammed Zahir Rashid MPharm, MRPharmS, PG ClinDip

Zahir has over 15 years clinical field experience; having been trained as a specialist oncology pharmacist with extensive experience in MHRA licensed aseptic services including CIVAS, batch manufacture of non-sterile products as well as feasibility assessment, aseptic manufacture, QA release and administration of Phase 1-3 clinical trials. Zahir has a wealth of experience within NHS commissioning, medicines information, formulary development and specialist procurement as well as system wide medicines shortage mitigation and multistakeholder engagement.

Mr Mohammed Rashid Khan MPharm, MRPharmS, PG ClinDip

Rashid has worked as Lead Pharmacist for Clinical Trials in two prominent NHS Trusts in London ensuring all trials of investigational medicinal products are conducted in accordance with ethics, law and clinical hospital policies. Rashid has experience working on Phase 1, 2, 3 and 4 studies in multiple clinical areas and understands the range of complexities modern clinical trials can have; thus ensuring clients will have the best experience in finding solutions to their operational and logistical problems. Rashid has managed a Pharmacy in a world renowned Early Phase Clinical Research Organisation, holding responsibility for the management of the Pharmacy and the preparation of IMP dosing for on-site administration.
