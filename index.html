What We Do

Manufacture

Oak Clinical Solutions are an MHRA licensed facility with an MIA (IMP) license to manufacture non-sterile IMPs

Sourcing

Worldwide vendor support in sourcing comparator products, commercial medicines, unlicensed medicines, pre-medications, non-investigational medicinal products and rescue medications

Clinical Trial Supplies

Oak Clinical Solutions can support all of your clinical trial needs by sourcing trial consumables.

GPhC Registered Pharmacy

Oak Clinical Solutions can also act as a satellite Pharmacy and provide a ‘dispense and deliver’ service for all trial participants

About Us

Founded in 2021 by a group of Pharmacists, Oak Clinical Solutions was formed as a UK-based Investigational Medicinal Product (IMP) manufacturing unit. Drawing on a combined 50 years of experience working in the NHS, Industry and community Pharmacy, Oak clinical Solutions can provide support to commercial and non-commercial Sponsors for all their clinical trial needs.
