Who We Are

Founded in 2021 by a group of Pharmacists, Oak Clinical Solutions was formed as a UK-based Investigational Medicinal Product (IMP) manufacturing unit. Drawing on a combined 50 years of experience working in the NHS, Industry and community Pharmacy, Oak clinical Solutions can provide support to commercial and non-commercial Sponsors for all their clinical trial needs.
