Clinical Services

Our team of specialists have a combined experience of over 50 years in clinical trial services


With extensive knowledge and experience in delivery of phase 1 to 4 clinical trials including IMPD and protocol development, translational research strategy and study setup

Manufacturing

Oak clinical specialise in primary and secondary packaging of IMPs from our dedicated MHRA licensed facility and are able to facilitate the manufacture of a variety of dosage forms

Packaging & Labelling

Our experienced team is ready to provide tailored solutions to your clinical trial packaging and labelling needs with specific expertise in delivery of blinded IMPs.

Sourcing

Worldwide vendor support in sourcing comparator (authorised) products, commercially available licensed medicines, unlicensed medicines, pre-medications, auxiliary medicines (NIMPs), rescue medicines and consumables.

Storage & Distribution

A dedicated UK based hub with the ability to handle and store ambient, fridge and frozen IMPs throughout the duration of the trial. A trusted distribution network via validated couriers in the UK and EU

Pharmacy Services

Oak Clinical Solutions can fully integrate with your trial protocol schedule to offer direct-to-patient dispensing.
